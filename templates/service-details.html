Listi Sindhupalchok

Listi, also known as Listikot, is a picturesque Sherpa village in Bhotekoshi Rural Municipality, Sindhupalchowk, around 59 km from Kathmandu. Located at about 2,300 m, it's famous for the historic Listi Mai (Ugrachandi Bhagwati) Temple, a major spiritual site. The village offers beautiful views of the Bhotekoshi River and surrounding hills, along with warm local hospitality. Though still recovering from the 2015 earthquake, Listi is a peaceful destination rich in culture, nature, and tradition.
